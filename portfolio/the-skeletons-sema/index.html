The Skeleton's Sema

A Dream

I had a dream.

I was standing in a misty space. Patterns of dark violet and light green light pervaded the mist.

A skeleton appeared. He approached me while constantly whirling.

He got closed to me and grabbed my hand. I started to whirl with him.

He was all bones, but his movement was soft and smooth. He had no lips, but like any other skeletons, he always seemed to be laughing and so cheerful. He had no eyes, but he was highly aware and his stare was intensed.

As I looked deeply, the darkness behind his “no-eyes” became my space. I kept zooming-in, and eventually I saw myself dancing. I became the skeleton.

A question came to me, “who’s dancing?” I couldn’t know, and the confusion woke me up.

Edited the skull, smoothen the clothes. I didn’t have the gas/fuel thing to smoothen the surface of the clothe, so I stopped here. My idea was expressed, spending more time to sharpen my (useless) sculpting skill to make the sculpture perfect would be unnecessary painful.
